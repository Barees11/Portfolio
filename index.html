I'm a Global Trade Compliance professional with 7+ years at EY and Deloitte, helping multinationals navigate export controls, customs classification, sanctions, and FTA qualification across multiple other sectors.

My edge is combining deep regulatory knowledge with data-driven automation — Power BI dashboards, Gen-AI monitoring tools, and AI/ML transformation projects that push classification accuracy to 90%. I hold an MBA (Finance) with distinction from MIT World Peace University.

Outside the office, I follow emerging technologies, AI/Gen-AI, investing, and global markets — which keeps me naturally attuned to how macro trends reshape trade policy. I believe compliance done right becomes a competitive advantage, not just a cost centre.
